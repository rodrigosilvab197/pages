Jalisco

Jalisco is one of the states that shape 32 federative entities of Mexico, it is located in the west of the country. Straw hat is one of the most typical states of the country, since it is a symbol of the tequila, mariachi music, settlement, between other things

Puntos de interés

In The state of Jalisco there are different points of interest of the best quality. Starting with the Pearl Of Guadalajara, where the Cathedral and the Historical Center are most seen places. Close to Guadalajara, are Tlaquepaque and Tonalá, known by its handmade beauties. In the same state you can fina: the Lake of Chapala, it is the biggest lake of Mexico; Tequila, producing the famous tequila; and on the coast, there is one of the most quoted beaches of Mexico, Port Vallarta

Guadalajara

Guadalajara is the capital of the state of Jalisco, it is the second biggest city of Mexico, its interest points are the Cathedral and the Historical Center. The Historical Center refers to the place where the city was founded, there many places meet an importance and immense beauty, which also they stand out for its architecture. The above mentioned places are, the already mentioned Cathedral, the Courthouse, Insitituto Cultural Hut, the Murdered Theater, the Square Guadalajara, the Square Liberation, the Rotunda of the Illustrious Men and the Square of Weapon.

Tonalá

Tonalá is a part of the Area Metropolotina of Guadalajara, it is a city that is a center of craft with national and international recognition, here the pottery, the ceramics one works to high temperature, wrought iron, papier-masher, embossed and blown glass. The places of major interest inside the city are: Santa Cruz of the Gardens, Hillocks of the Camichín, Fifth Catalina, Golden Hillock, between others.

Tlaquepaque

Tlaquepaque is, also, a city that shapes the Metropolitan area of Guadalajara. This city is characterized by the crafts of pottery, textiles and blown glass. Its streets and good walkers are adorned of diverse Large houses of last century. The most important attractions are: Parish of Saint Peter, Gentlemanly Garden, Sanctum of the Solitude, Municipal Palace, The Parián, Good walker Independencia, Cultural center The Refuge and Museum Pantaleón Panduro.

Lago de Chapala

The lake Chapala is located in the East of Guadalajara, has an entire capacity of almost 8,000 cubic hectometers and an entire surface of 114,659 hectares of which Jalisco hat occupies 86 % and Michoacán only de 14%. In Straw hat the municipalities that are on the beach of the lake are: Plate it, Jocotepec, Ajijic, between others. The above mentioned municipalities are provided with hotel areas and of restaurants, in addition to with certain crafts. This region is favorite of some Americans and Canadians, since there have developed areas of retirement of North Americans.

Tequila

Tequila is located to the north of Guadalajara, is thought a "magic town". Tequila is known for naming him the tequila, to the being one of the territories where this one takes place. The main producers of tequila who are inside the city are: José Cuervo, Distilling Blond, Orendain, Sauza, between others. In the town it is possible to go to tours of the different tequila producers, where there is appreciated the process used for the creation of the drink. Also, it is possible to taste and to buy of internationally famous tequila.

Puerto Vallarta

Puerto Vallarta its found on the coast west of Mexico, it is the most important second economic area of the state of Jalisco hat and the most important third port of Mexico, also it is one of the most visited places of the country. Port Vallarta is provided with numerous hotel areas, including hotels category diamond, in addition to which it is provided with an International Airport. In Puerto Vallarta a levee exists with different restaurants, shops, bars, and a magnificent sight to the Pacific Ocean.
